White Label

XCoinCH is white label enabled. The white label licensees can focus on marketing without massive development costs.

Small DeFi institutions may be interested in this mode.

Advantages

  1. No need to develop smart contracts.

  2. Distributed architecture makes integration easier. WEB-based, no high tech threshold.

  3. Low cost to list cryptocurrencies.

  4. Flexible to determine the exchange fee.

  5. Serves as a correspondent bank in inter-banks transferring.

  6. Order book based matching, no liquidity pool required.

  7. Credit risk dispersion.

Who is Eligible to be a White Label Licensee?

If you have had a license under the regulation of a certain jurisdiction, then you are permission-free and can be eligible to request a white label immediately.

If you have no license, that doesn’t matter. We will review your profile and due diligence. Regardless of the scale of your institution, Small institutions can be white label licensees as well.

How to Request for a White Label License?

Please click this link to access our request form: XCoinCH Dashboard

And then click the menu item “White Label” on the left sidebar, you can see a form on the main panel. Please fill in the form and submit it. We will review and revert to you as soon as possible.
