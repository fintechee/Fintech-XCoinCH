Coin Listing

XCoinCH is white label enabled. The white label licensees can determine who is eligible to list coins.

Small DeFi institutions may be interested in this mode.

Advantages

  1. No need to launch IEO.

  2. Distributed architecture makes integration easier. WEB-based, no high tech threshold.

  3. Low cost to list cryptocurrencies.

  4. Order book based matching, no liquidity pool required.

  5. Credit risk dispersion.

Who is Eligible to List Cryptocurrencies(Tokens)?

Our white label licensees determine the policies on whether to accept coin-listing requests.

How to Request for Cryptocurrencies(Tokens) Listing?

Please click this link to access our request form: XCoinCH Dashboard

And then click the menu item “Listing” on the left sidebar, you can see a form on the main panel. Please fill in the form and submit it. Our white label licensee will review and revert to you as soon as possible.
