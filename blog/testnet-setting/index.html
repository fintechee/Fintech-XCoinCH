Scatter(EOS-based wallet) and Metamask(Ethereum-based wallet) are applied on XCoinCH. Let’s see how to configure the settings.

If you use the MainNet, then it’s not required to configure any settings except the accounts and the private keys importing.

So, please note that this post is just a guide about how to use our platform to exchange on the TestNet.

TestNet Setting

Scatter

Scatter is used to sign transactions for transferring tokens based on EOS platform.

  • Host: jungle3.cryptolions.io
  • Port: 443
  • Protocol: https
  • Chain ID: e70aaab8997e1dfce58fbfac80cbbb8fecec7b99cf982a9444273cbc64c41473

JUNGLE and EOS are available tokens(cryptocurrencies) on the TestNet of EOS. If you want to try other tokens, please request listing on our platform: XCoinCH

Metamask

Metamask is used to sign transactions for transferring tokens based on Ethereum platform.

  • Host: rinkeby.infura.io/v3/b2e3502561ba4afaaa8a05c030e99e4a
  • Port: 443
  • Protocol: https

WEENUS, XEENUS, and ETH are available tokens(cryptocurrencies) on the TestNet of Ethereum. If you want to try other tokens, please request listing on our platform: XCoinCH
